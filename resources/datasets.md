# Datasets for Generative Tactile AI Research

![Last Updated](https://img.shields.io/badge/last%20updated-November%202025-blue)

This page lists **datasets** relevant to tactile accessibility, haptics, and generative AI.  
It covers resources for tactile graphics, vibrotactile synthesis, and multimodal tactile-visual learning.

## Datasets

| Dataset | Year | Domain | Access / License | Description |
|:---------|:------|:--------|:-----------------|:-------------|
| **TactileNet Dataset** | 2025 | Tactile graphics (text-to-image, diffusion) | [Hugging Face: MaiAShaaban/TactileNet](https://huggingface.co/datasets/MaiAShaaban/TactileNet) | 32k AI-generated embossing-ready images across 66 classes, with 7,050 expert-rated samples and accessibility compliance metadata. |
| **Text-Guided RGB→Tactile Maps Dataset** | 2025 | Tactile maps (text-guided translation) | [Carleton University Repository](https://hdl.handle.net/20.500.14718/43558) | 1,845 RGB maps paired with multiple tactile counterparts, totaling 9,800 triplets (RGB map, tactile map, prompt). |
| **Tactile Map Street-View Dataset** | 2024 | Cartographic tactile map synthesis | [https://doi.org/10.20383/103.0797](https://doi.org/10.20383/103.0797) | Dataset includes 6 features (streets, highways, buildings, parks, bodies of water, medical facilities) across 4 countries (Australia, Canada, UK, US) at zoom levels 15–18, with 5k train and 500 test pairs each, plus 1k world cities test maps. Licensed under CC BY 4.0; dataset size approximately 1.99 GB. |
| **Residue-Fusion Cross-Modal Dataset** | 2021 | Visual↔Tactile cross-modal | [arXiv:2107.05468](https://arxiv.org/abs/2107.05468) (on request) | Texture–vibration pairs used for Residue-Fusion GAN training and perceptual loss benchmarking; data available upon author request. |
| **Vibrotactile Texture Dataset** | 2019 | Vibrotactile pattern generation | [EuroHaptics 2019 paper](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-3-319-93399-3_3) (on request) | Image–vibration pattern pairs for conditional GAN-based texture-to-vibration synthesis; early open benchmark for haptic pattern generation. |
