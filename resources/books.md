This page lists foundational and practical books related to **tactile graphics, haptic design, and accessibility** — covering design guidelines, tactile communication theory, and human perception.  
These references complement the papers listed in [`data/papers.csv`](../data/papers.csv).

## Core References

| Title | Authors / Organization | Year | Focus / Notes | Link |
|:------|:-----------------------|:------|:---------------|:------|
| **Guidelines and Standards for Tactile Graphics** | Braille Authority of North America (BANA) | 2010 | The definitive reference for tactile design conventions — line thickness, texture patterns, labeling rules. | [BANA TG Guidelines](http://www.brailleauthority.org/tg/web-manual/index.html) |
| **Guide to Designing Tactile Illustrations for Children’s Books** | American Printing House for the Blind (APH) | 2008 | Practical reference for tactile book design and early childhood accessibility. | [APH Publication](https://books.google.co.jp/books?id=XYlaywAACAAJ) |
| **Tactile Graphics: An Overview and Resource Guide** | John A. Gardner | 1996 | Early synthesis of tactile communication methods and transcribing techniques. | [ITD Journal](http://itd.athenpro.org/volume3/number4/article2.html) |


## Haptics and Perception

| Title | Authors | Year | Focus / Notes | Link |
|:------|:--------|:------|:--------------|:------|
| **Human Haptic Perception: Basics and Applications** | Martin Grunwald (ed.) | 2008 | Classic edited volume summarizing haptic perception, texture discrimination, and tactile interface design. | [SpringerLink](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-3-7643-7612-3) |
| **Haptic Rendering: Foundations, Algorithms, and Applications** | Ming C. Lin, Miguel A. Otaduy | 2008 | Core reference for force-feedback and vibrotactile interface modeling. | [Digital ACM Library](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.5555/1386770) |
| **The Handbook of Multisensory Processes** | Calvert, Spence, Stein | 2004 | Covers multimodal perception — relevant for integrating touch and vision in tactile design. | [MIT Press](https://direct.mit.edu/books/edited-volume/5424/The-Handbook-of-Multisensory-Processes) |
